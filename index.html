Our Story

Built by Barbers,
For the Community

Ember & Oak started in 2011 with one chair, one barber, and a simple belief: a great haircut should feel like a ritual, not a transaction. Thirteen years later, we're still at it — and still loving every single cut.

We're a neighborhood shop through and through. Half our clients have been coming since day one. Their kids come now too. That's the whole point — a place where everyone feels at home.
